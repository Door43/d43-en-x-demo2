====== 40. Jesus Is Crucified ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-01.jpg}}

And after that the soldiers of the governor had scorned him, they led him for to be crucified and constrained him to take his cross.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-02.jpg}}

And they came into a place that is called Golgotha and after that they had crucified him. But Jesus said, Father, forgive them, for they know not what they do. And Pilate wrote a title, and set on the cross ; and it was written, king of Jews.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-03.jpg}}

The soldiers took his clothes and said, Cast we lot, whose it is; that the scripture be fulfilled, saying, They parted my clothes to them, and on my cloak they cast lot.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-04.jpg}}

Then two thieves were crucified with him, one on the right half, and one on the left half. And one of these thieves that hanged, blasphemed him. Soothly the tother answering, blamed him, saying, Neither thou dreadest God, thou that art in the same damnation? And he said to Jesus, Lord, have mind of me, when thou comest into thy kingdom. And Jesus said to him, This day thou shalt be with me in paradise.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-05.jpg}}

Also the high priests scorned him each to other with the scribes, they blasphemed him, moving their heads, and said, Fie to thee; save thou thyself; if thou art the Son of God, come down of the cross.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-06.jpg}}

And it was almost the sixth hour, and darknesses were made on all the earth into the ninth hour.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-07.jpg}}

Then Jesus said, It is ended. And when his head was bowed down, he sent out the spirit. And lo! the veil of the temple was rent atwo, from the highest to the lowest. And the earth shook, and the stones were cloven


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-08.jpg}}

But the centurion that stood over against saw, and he said, Verily, this man was God's Son.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-40-09.jpg}}

But after these things Joseph prayed Pilate, that he should take away the body of Jesus, for that he was a disciple of Jesus and Nicodemus came also. And they took the body of Jesus, and bound it in linen clothes and laid it in his new burial, that he had hewn in a stone; and he wallowed a great stone to the door of the burial, and went away.

//A Bible story from: Matthew 27:27-61; Mark 15:16-47; Luke 23:26-56; John 19:17-42//
