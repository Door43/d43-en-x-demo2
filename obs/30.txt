====== 30. Jesus Feeds Five Thousand People ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-01.jpg}}

They left and walked around to other villages telling other people about Jesus. When Jesus' close friends returned, they told Jesus all that had happened. Jesus took just them with Him on a ship to a quiet desert place.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-02.jpg}}

Many people recognized Jesus and saw him leave.  The people left their towns and gathered near where he was.  When Jesus came out, he saw a large crowd of people.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-03.jpg}}

The multitude had about five thousand men, beside women and children. He was moved with compassion toward them, because they were as sheep not having a shepherd: and he began to teach them many things and he healed their sick.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-04.jpg}}

And when it was evening, his disciples came to him, saying, This is a desert place, and the time is now past; send the multitude away, that they may go into the villages, and buy themselves victuals.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-05.jpg}}

Jesus said to them, "Give them some food to eat." His disciples said, "We don't have enough food. We only have five loaves of bread and two fish."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-06.jpg}}

So he directed his  men to have everybody sit down in groups of fifty.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-07.jpg}}

Then he took the five loaves and the two fishes, and looking up to heaven, he blessed them.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-08.jpg}}

Then he brake, and gave to the disciples to set before the multitude. And they did eat, and were all filled.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-30-09.jpg}}

And they took up twelve baskets full of the fragments, and of the fishes.



// A Bible story from: Matthew 14:13-21; Mark 6:31-44; Luke 9:10-17; John 6:5-15 //
