====== 41. God Raises Jesus from the Dead ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-01.jpg}}

And on the tother day, that is after pask even, the princes of priests and Pharisees came together to Pilate, and said, Sir, we have mind, that that beguiler said yet living, After three days I shall rise again to life. Therefore command thou, that the sepulchre be kept into the third day; lest his disciples come, and steal him, and say to the people, He hath risen from death.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-02.jpg}}

Pilate said to them, Ye have the keeping; go ye, keep ye as ye know how. And they went forth, and kept the sepulchre, marking the stone, with soldiers.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-03.jpg}}

But in the eventide of the sabbath, that beginneth to shine in the first day of the week, some women came, and to see the sepulchre bringing sweet smelling ointments, to come and to anoint Jesus. 


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-04.jpg}}

And lo! there was made a great earth-shaking; for the angel of the Lord came down from heaven, and approached, and turned away the stone, and sat thereon. And for dread of him the keepers were afeared, and they were made as dead men.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-05.jpg}}

But the angel answered, and said to the women, Do not ye dread, for I know that ye seek Jesus, that was crucified; he is not here, for he is risen, as he said; come ye, and see ye the place, where the Lord was laid. And they went in, and found not the body of the Lord Jesus.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-06.jpg}}

And go ye soon, and say ye to his disciples, that he is risen. And lo! he shall go before you into Galilee; there ye shall see him.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-07.jpg}}

And they went out soon from the burials, with dread and great joy, running to tell to his disciples.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-41-08.jpg}}

And lo! Jesus met them, and said, Hail ye. And they approached, and held his feet, and worshipped him. Then Jesus said to them, Do not ye dread; go ye, tell ye to my brethren, that they go into Galilee; there they shall see me.

//A Bible story from: Matthew 27:62-28:15; Mark 16:1-11; Luke 24:1-12; John 20:1-18//
