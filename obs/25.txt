====== 25. Satan Tempts Jesus to Do Wrong ======



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-01.jpg?direct&}}

Right away the Holy Spirit led Jesus into the uninhabited land, where Satan tempted him for 40 days to do evil. During all that time, Jesus didn't eat anything.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg?direct&}}

And Satan tempted him by saying, "if you are the Son of God, tell this stone to turn into bread."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-03.jpg?direct&}}

Jesus resonded to him by saying "It is written that man cannot live by only eating bread, but by knowing and doing every word of God."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-04.jpg?direct&}}

Satan led Jesus into the holy city and brought him to the peak of the roof of the temple. Satan said, "If you are the Son of God, jump to the ground. The holy book says God's angels (heavenly servants) are watching over you, and will rescue you and keep you from any injury".



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-05.jpg?direct&}}

Jesus said to him, "It is also written in the scriptures, 'Do not tempt God your master."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-06.jpg?direct&}}

Again, Satan showed him all the countries of the world, and the awesomness of them, and said, "I will give you all of these if you will bow down and worship me."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg?direct&}}

Then Jesus said to him, "Leave Satan!, for it is written that you should only serve and worship God, your master."



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-08.jpg?direct&}}

Satan left, and angels came and cared for Jesus.



// A Bible story from: Matthew 4:1-11, Mark 1:12-13, Luke 4:1-13 //
