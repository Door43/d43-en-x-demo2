====== 25. Satan Tempts Jesus ======

{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-01.jpg}}

And immediately the spirit driveth him into the wilderness. Being forty days tempted of the devil. And in those days he did eat nothing.


{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-02.jpg}}

And the devil said unto him, If thou be the Son of God, command this stone that it be made bread.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-03.jpg}}

And Jesus answered him, saying, It is written, That man shall not live by bread alone, but by every word of God.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-04.jpg}}

Then the devil taketh him up into the holy city, and setteth him on a pinnacle of the temple. And saith unto him, If thou be the Son of God, cast thyself down: for it is written, He shall give his angels charge concerning thee: and in their hands they shall bear thee up, lest at any time thou dash thy foot against a stone.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-05.jpg}}

Jesus said unto him, It is written again, Thou shalt not tempt the Lord thy God.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-06.jpg}}

Again, the devil sheweth him all the kingdoms of the world, and the glory of them; and saith unto him, All these things will I give thee, if thou wilt fall down and worship me.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-07.jpg}}

Then saith Jesus unto him, Get thee hence, Satan: for it is written, Thou shalt worship the Lord thy God, and him only shalt thou serve.



{{https://api.unfoldingword.org/obs/jpg/1/en/360px/obs-en-25-08.jpg}}

Then the devil leaveth him, and, behold, angels came and ministered unto him.

//A Bible story from: Matthew 4:1-11; Mark 1:12-13; Luke 4:1-13//
